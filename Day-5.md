### Summary

Today, I learned about **Data Visualization**, which is the graphical representation of data to identify patterns, trends, relationships, and insights. The trainer explained how visualization makes it easier to understand large datasets, detect outliers, compare groups, and support decision-making.

We explored the **Matplotlib** and **Seaborn** libraries and created different types of charts, including Histograms, Box Plots, Count Plots, Bar Charts, Pie Charts, Line Charts, Violin Plots, Scatter Plots, Heatmaps, Pair Plots, and Area Charts. We also learned about **Correlation** and how a Heatmap helps visualize relationships between numerical variables.

### Key Learning
- Importance of data visualization in data analysis.
- Difference between Matplotlib and Seaborn.
- Creating different types of charts for numerical and categorical data.
- Understanding correlation using Heatmaps.
- Visualizing relationships between variables for better insights.
