### Summary

Today was the first day of AI & Machine Learning training. I learned the basic concepts of AI and Machine Learning and understood how they are used to solve real-world problems. The trainer explained the importance of **Data Exploration**, which helps us understand a dataset before building machine learning models.

We also discussed different **data types** such as integer, float, string, and boolean, along with **data modalities** like text, images, audio, video, and tabular data. A real-life example of house price prediction was used to explain how data exploration helps identify important features for analysis.

### Key Learning
- Basics of AI and Machine Learning.
- Importance of data exploration.
- Different types of data used in ML.
- Understanding various data modalities.
- Real-life applications of AI and ML.
